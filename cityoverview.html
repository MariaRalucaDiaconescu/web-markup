City overview

Hoofddorp is located in the province North Holland in the Netherlands. The city offers a great experience for students! While being located near cities with universities for international students, it is far from the hustle of a metropolis.

Some places that you will inevitably come across as a student

  1. The campus Opaallaand
  2. The city center
  3. Learning centers

If you are really interested studying in the Netherlands, don’t miss out browsing through the website!

The campus Opaallaand

The campus Opaalland is a grouping of buildings with self-contained houses, with a few hundred homes.There are facilities such as parking spaces, bicycle sheds and picnic spots. The campus is a suitable location for people who study close to this area. Especially because it is conveniently located to transport links. Thus it is easy to reach Amsterdam, Haarlem and Leiden where there are schools for international students.

If you want to live here you must find your accomodation a few months before beginning your study programme. You can do this through various websites where you can search for student housing. Here is a helpful link.

The city center

It is probably the place where you’d mostly go shopping or hang out with friends. It is a vivid part of Hoofddorp and it offers o wide range of services such as restaurants, shops and the cinema.

Close nearby is the Stadspark. It is an amazing park for doing sports and is situated within short distance from the city mall!

In the shopping center, you can find more than 200 shops, from big to small! Because of this fact, shopping can take a lot of time. You may find the weekend the best time for shopping, also due to the many terraces open during the weekend. Or you could delight yourself with the most recent movies at the last lightning bolt of cinema, CineMeerse! The cinema is open every day of the week. If you’re a cinema enthusiast, visit this page!

Learning centers

Various training centers in the area offer the opportunity to learn about topics such as foreign languages, IT and driving.

International students have the possibility to learn Dutch for free! At the Taalhuis from Hoofddorp you can get help from a language coach. You can also receive advice about which books you can use. This you have chances to be successfull as fast as possible in learning Dutch! You can also take part in group activities with other students, to learn the Dutch language more easily. The language walks that are organized once a week are a good way to progress!

You may also have to deal with the computer during your studies. And you might need help on a certain topic. The best option is to seek the resources in a public bibliotheek! The same applies if you want to study for a driving licence! These places offer the option to take online courses or to work and study on-site. The possibilities to learn are endless!
